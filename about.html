About

My Story

I’m a New York City based UX/UI designer. My user-centric, empathetic philosophy has brought me to UX/UI design and has given me the skills needed to create aesthetically pleasing, simple to use and pixel-perfect designs. I have a background in sales and marketing working in Real Estate, the recruiting space and the fashion industry. In my past customer-facing roles, I’ve focused on building relationships, collaborating with others and providing top-notch experiences to clients which has informed my understanding of how important a user experience is. This has given me a unique skillset which allows me to quickly connect with and gain insights from users to facilitate the best experiences for them. I’m always looking for the next opportunity to help others build products out of their ideas and deliver user-friendly designs.
